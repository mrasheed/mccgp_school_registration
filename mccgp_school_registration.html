Are you able to pay full tuition?

Tuition must be paid in full to the Office at MCCGP at the beginning of the school year. Failure to do so will suspend your child(ren) from attending until payment is made. If full tuition cannot be paid, then a discount may be applied on a case-by-case basis. You must apply to the MCCGP Office to discuss this matter in detail, and complete the required documentation. Payments can be made either by check, cash, or credit/debit card. Please be sure to take a receipt as proof of payment, if you are paying by cash.
